# Box-Office-Movies-Revenue Analysis:

## **Problem Statement:**
The global movie industry has seen major shifts in audience preferences, international market performance, and genre popularity from 2000 to 2024. However, stakeholders often lack a centralized, data-driven view of how domestic revenue, foreign revenue, production countries, genres, and languages contribute to overall box-office success.  
This analysis aims to uncover key revenue patterns and provide actionable insights that can guide movie studios, distributors, investors, and streaming platforms toward better strategic decisions.

---

## **Project Description:**
This project provides insights into the revenue trends of trending box-office movies from **2000 to 2024**, based on different factors such as domestic and foreign revenue, production countries, genres, and languages.

# **Recommendations:**

### **For Filmmakers:**
- Focus on Horror & Comedy domestically.  
- Invest more in Action & Adventure films for international markets.

### **For Distributors:**
- Prioritize U.S., U.K., and China for distribution deals.  
- Localize content for Cantonese, Japanese, and Korean speakers.

### **For Investors:**
- Invest in high-performing genres: Action, Adventure, Sci-Fi.

### **For Streaming Platforms:**
- Acquire English-language blockbusters.  
- Expand non-English content for niche markets.
